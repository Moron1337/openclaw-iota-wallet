# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions first.
- Keep discussions actionable and relevant to the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, doxxing, threats, or
deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for
violations.

If you observe violations, open an issue with clear evidence or contact a
maintainer privately.
